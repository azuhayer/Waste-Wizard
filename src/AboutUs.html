About Us

Recycling properly can be difficult, and many people dont know where to start. Waste Wizard aims to educate people about practical and sustainable ways to dispose of waste. Built using Facebook's Detectron2, the main feature of this site is a trash detector which allows users to upload an image of a waste product to easily categorize items that might be otherwise difficult to classify. The ever present threat of climate change has motivated our team to create a place where people can quickly get guidance on how to classify a waste product.
